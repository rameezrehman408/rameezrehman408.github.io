Public Key Cryptography standards (PKCS)

  1. PKCS# 1: Raw Signature standard. This is then used with PKCS# 7 to make it meaningful.

  2. PKCS# 7: Cryptographic Message Syntax Standard Standard describes general syntax for data that may have cryptography applied to it, such as digital signatures and digital envelopes. Also known as CMS - Cryptographic Message Syntax (RFC 5652).
